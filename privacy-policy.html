Privacy Policy

We at Jeantubo Essentials Store value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data every time you shop with us.

Information We Collect

To fulfill your orders and provide the best possible service, we may request personal details such as your name, contact information, delivery address, and payment preferences. We only ask for information that’s necessary to process your order and keep you informed about your purchase.

How We Use Your Data
  • To process and deliver your orders accurately and efficiently.
  • To communicate with you regarding your purchases, order updates, and customer support.
  • To send you updates about new products, promotions, and important store announcements (with your consent).
  • To improve our products, services, and website experience.
How We Protect Your Privacy

We use secure systems and trusted payment methods to keep your information safe. Your details are never shared with external parties unless it is required to complete your order (e.g., courier delivery). We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information for marketing purposes.

Your Choices and Rights

You can request to view, update, or delete your personal information at any time by contacting us. If you prefer not to receive promotional updates, you can opt out easily via the link in our emails or by letting us know directly.

Updates to Our Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our operations or to comply with regulations. We'll notify you of any important changes via our website or direct communication.
